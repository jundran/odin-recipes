Spaghetti

Description

Combine noodles, fresh tomatoes and water, then simmer for perfectly cooked pasta and a luxurious sauce all in one pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon 12 oz. spaghetti 2
  • Cloves garlic, thinly sliced 1
  • Small red onion, thinly sliced 1/2 c.
  • Dry white wine 2 tbsp.
  • Olive oil Kosher salt and pepper 1 1/2 pt.
  • Assorted color cherry or grape tomatoes, halved 1/2 c.
  • Parsley leaves, roughly chopped 1 c.
  • Basil leaves, roughly chopped
  • Grated Parmesan or vegan Parmesan alternative, for serving

Steps

  1. From lemon, peel 4 strips of zest and thinly slice, then squeeze 2 tablespoons juice.
  2. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, place spaghetti (it should lie flat on the bottom). Add garlic, onion, lemon juice, wine, oil, 4 cups water, 3/4 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Bring to a boil and boil gently, stirring frequently, 5 minutes.
  3. Fold in tomatoes and continue to boil gently until pasta is al dente and nearly all the liquid has been absorbed (absorption will continue).
  4. Stir in lemon zest and parsley, then fold in basil. Serve with grated Parmesan if desired.
